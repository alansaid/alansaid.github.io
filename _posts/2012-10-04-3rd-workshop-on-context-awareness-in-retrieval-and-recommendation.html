In 2013 there'll be plenty of reason's for going to Rome. Two of these are UMAP 2013 in June, and WSDM 2013 in February. And for the first time in conjunction with WSDM, we're organizing the Workshop on Context-awareness in Retrieval and Recommendation.

 

 

After having been organized in conjunction with IUI 2011 and IUI2012, the workshop is now changing venues to WSDM as a results of the discussion at the last instalment of the workshop.

The 2013 edition is organized by:

  • Matthias Böhmer - DFKI
  • Ernesto De Luca - FH Potsdam
  • Alan Said - TU Berlin
  •  Jaime Teevan - Microsoft Research


Be sure to check out the website for more information about submission and important dates www.carr-workshop.org
